Supported Breeds & Characteristics

The model recognizes 15 Indian cattle and buffalo breeds across Zebu, draft, and riverine categories.

Model Architecture

EfficientNet-B3

CNN backbone extracting 1536-D local texture and morphological features from the input image.

ViT-B/16

Vision Transformer extracting 768-D global attention features, capturing long-range body structure.

Hybrid Fusion

CNN + ViT features are concatenated (2304-D) and passed through a 512-unit FC head with dropout.

ConvNeXt Disease

Separate ConvNeXt-Tiny model for disease triage: Foot-&-Mouth, Lumpy Skin, and Healthy classification.
